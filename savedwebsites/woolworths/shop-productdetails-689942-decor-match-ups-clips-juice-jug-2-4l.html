Product Details

Flavour your water with the Decor Match-ups Clips 2.4 L Fruit Infuser Jug. With a removable strainer that catches pips and pulp its so easy to create delicious healthy flavoured water. Add berries, citrus, cucumber and herbs into the base of the jug and add water, then place the strainer inside the jug and apply lid. Jug features secure no-leak clips and an airtight seal for freshness. • Removable strainer catches pips and pulp. • Designed to fit inside standard fridge doors. • Airtight seal to keep ingredients fresher for longer. • Secure clips. • Leak-proof seal. • Pour spout designed for easy smooth pouring. • Easy grip sturdy handle for smooth pouring. • Simple to use two piece rocking lid. • Large 2.4L capacity. • All parts are dishwasher safe for easy cleaning. • Microwave safe. • Freezer safe. • BPA Free
